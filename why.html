About IEEE

IEEE is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity.

  • Founded in 1963 from the merger of AIEE and IRE
  • Over 400,000 members in 160+ countries
  • Publishes 30% of world's literature in electrical engineering, electronics, and computer science
  • Develops over 1,300 active standards
  • Sponsors more than 1,800 conferences annually
  • Provides educational resources and career development tools

IEEE Computer Society

The IEEE Computer Society is the premier organization for computer professionals, with over 60,000 members worldwide.

  • Founded in 1946 as part of the American Institute of Electrical Engineers
  • Leading provider of technical information, community services, and educational programs
  • Publishes 17 peer-reviewed magazines and journals
  • Sponsors 200+ technical conferences and workshops each year
  • Develops and maintains over 200 active standards
  • Provides professional certification programs

Career Advancement

IEEE provides numerous resources to help you advance in your career and stay competitive in the job market.

  • Access to the IEEE Job Site with thousands of tech positions
  • Professional development courses and certifications
  • Resume building and career coaching services
  • Networking events with industry leaders
  • Leadership opportunities in local chapters and committees
  • Mentorship programs for students and young professionals
  • Recognition through awards and scholarships
